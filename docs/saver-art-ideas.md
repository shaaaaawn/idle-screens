# Saver Art Ideas — 10 High-Level Briefs

In the spirit of `triadisches-ballett` (ballet channel): a single strong artwork/concept per scene, every layer traceable back to it, a restricted palette (3–5 colors), and motion that serves the mood rather than showing off. Each brief below is self-contained and can be fed to an agent to expand into a full SaverSpec.

## 1. Klimt — Der Kuss (Golden Phase)
A gilded meadow dissolving into ornament. Deep umber-to-black background; large soft gold circles (`blend: lighter`, low alpha) clustered at center like the couple's embrace; small geometric text sprites (■ ● ▲ ◆) in gold, bronze, and malachite rising slowly like the flower meadow at the cliff edge; tiny warm sparks drifting with long trails like gold leaf flaking. Palette: `#d4af37`, `#8b6914`, `#2e5e4e`, ivory. Seed: 1908.

## 2. Hokusai — The Great Wave, deconstructed
The wave as pure rhythm. Indigo-black gradient sea; one slow large orbit of foam-white soft circles arcing across the upper third (the claw of the wave); layers of tiny cerulean and prussian-blue dots bouncing gently like spray; faint grey static specks for distant boats; a small persistent pale disc low on the horizon (Fuji). Motion asymmetrical — everything sweeps left-to-right. Palette: `#1a3a5c`, `#f4f1e8`, `#0d1b2a`.

## 3. Hilma af Klint — The Ten Largest
Spiritual diagrams breathing. Very dark plum ground; a few large soft circles in dusty rose, ochre, and pale blue slowly growing and pulsing (her "swan" and "altarpiece" discs); fine white dots orbiting at many radii like her spiral diagrams; occasional text glyphs (✳ ❋ ♾) spinning slowly at low alpha; everything calm, sacred, slow — periods of 8–15s. Seed: 1907.

## 4. Tarkovsky — Nostalghia's candle
A single point of devotion. Near-black warm background; one small warm-white flame-disc with strong grow/pulse wobble, static at center; faint smoke-like soft grey circles rising very slowly; sparse embers in amber drifting with long fading trails; a wide, almost invisible halo (`blend: lighter`, alpha 0.05) that breathes on a 10s cycle. Minimalism as tension — most of the screen is darkness.

## 5. Deep-sea bioluminescence — the midnight zone
Light where light shouldn't exist. True black background; rare (5–10) electric-cyan and violet soft circles that pulse in sudden bursts (pulse amp high, period long) like jellyfish flashes; tiny green sparks with very long trails drifting downward slowly (marine snow); one or two slow orbiting larger glows. Negative space is the subject: 90% darkness, 10% miracle. Palette: `#00e5ff`, `#7c4dff`, `#69f0ae`.

## 6. Mondrian — Broadway Boogie Woogie
The grid as jazz. Not static blocks but motion: small squares in pure red/yellow/blue bouncing energetically along mostly horizontal and vertical angles, dense like taxi traffic; thin white/grey dashes drifting in long perpendicular trails (the grid lines implied); pale warm-cream background instead of black — a daytime saver. Higher motionIntensity, syncopated speeds, short trails. Seed: 1943.

## 7. Turner — The Fighting Temeraire
Light devouring form. Background gradient of bruised orange, gold, and violet drifting slowly; a huge soft pale-gold sun-disc low and off-center, barely above alpha 0.15; the ghost ship: a cluster of very faint white-grey soft circles drifting in unison leftward at almost-zero speed; amber glints shimmering on a lower "water" region band with gentle pulse. Everything near-invisible, dissolving, mournful.

## 8. Kusama — Infinity Nets / obliteration room
Pattern as trance. Deep red (`#a00000`) or deep violet ground; hundreds of small dots in one contrasting color (white or acid yellow) in near-uniform distribution, each pulsing slightly out of phase so the whole field seems to breathe and ripple; a few larger orbs drifting slowly through the field like pumpkins; no trails, no fast motion — pure repetition and hallucination through density.

## 9. O'Keeffe — a flower at night
One form, monumental. Dark indigo ground; a few very large, very soft circles in layered magenta, coral, and deep rose overlapping at center with `blend: lighter` so their intersections bloom brighter (petal folds); slow grow cycles making the flower seem to open and close over ~20s; tiny dew-sparks orbiting close to the bloom; outer darkness intact. The scale is the drama.

## 10. Voyager — the Pale Blue Dot
Cosmic humility. Absolute black; hundreds of pin-sharp static stars at alpha 0.1–0.4; one small pale-blue soft disc at 0.6, 0.5 with a faint pulsing halo — Earth from 6 billion km; extremely slow diagonal drift of a few dust motes with long faint trails (the sunbeam artifact from the actual photo); maybe one slow amber glint passing every so often. Vast, still, humbling. Seed: 1990.
